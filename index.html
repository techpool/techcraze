MANUAL ROBOTICS

Manual robotics is where the participant needs to control his/her own bot via a remote. The bot can't go on it's own. This is an event where the participant not only needs to make a great design but also needs to have great proficiency in the control mechanism of the bot so as to pass all the hurdles and take away the prize

We have a wide range of events for MANUAL ROBOTICS, ranging from a bot which has to pass the real world terrains to a bot where it has to play carrom and soccer. We even have an event where the bot needs to stay afloat over water and move the obstacles to proper positions, so for this event you need to be extra careful about the insulation.

  • Terrain Exp.
  • Pacific Rim
  • Robo Soccer

Autonomous Robots

Autonomous Robot deals with those robots which does not need any user control. All you need to do is program your bot and leave the rest to itself.

We have three different events for autonomous robots, starting from Blackolyte which is a line follower event, where the bot needs to follow a line of particular colour and reach the finish line. Save the soldiers is where your bot needs detect mines to save soldiers of predefined colours and shoot the enemies. The third event which is Shield Bot is an event where the bot needs to detect and push objects from a rotating disk.

Ideate Yourself!

Ideate is an event which checks the power of new ideas. In these events we are going to test your power of imagination. One idea can change the whole future. So come forward and put forth your own futuristic idea. Maybe we have our Indian Zuckerberg right amongst us!

We have two events for Ideate, one named Ideathon where you need to put forward you own business idea and another is Innov Gallery where the participant needs to present an innovative model of the stuff that he thinks can change the future.

Camera Clicks!

We all find it amazing that many artists are hidden amongst we engineers. Many of us have been dreaming to become photographers and film makers. So this is your chance to showcase your talent and come forward with great new concepts.

We have three great events for the Camera Clicks! One is Photography where obviously you need to submit your best clicks to bag the first prize and the other two include video shoots. One is where you need to shoot your own original Short Film and the other one is where you need to make an original Documentry about some current scenario around us.

Tech Design

Tech Design is all about your professional designing skills. One might have imaginations of great building structures and awesome circuits (provided you are a geek) but they are of no use unless you have it laid out in front of you with proper dimensions and exact measurements. So here we are with an event to showcase your professional design skills

We have a wide range of events for Tech Design has got two sections in it. One deals with CAD design where all your ideas needs to be designed in a Computer and the other with circuits designs which is all about making awesome circuits with less of fuzzy wires and more great functionalities.

  • CAD Design
  • Circuit Design

Xant

This is a call for all the coders around us. We all know what we do over here. Three primary things we need to do here, first is we code, second is we code again and last is we code all over again.

In this event all you need to do is code out the problem in the shortest time with correct outputs and shortest running time. So not only do you need to make your brain work fast but also need to use the correct algorithm in order to win over other great coders.

Gaming

The excitement can range from the rushing cars of NFS Most Wanted to the awesome kicks of Lionel Messi in FIFA 11. The fun doesn't ends here, we have the game for the bro gangs which is none other that Counter Strike. Never has competing been more fun than this!

Bridge Building

Bridge Building is where engineers gets to show their learning and how they will be changing India with greater connectivity in future.

This is where you need to build bridges right out of Ice Cream sticks, using some other stuffs like glue, cellotape, etc. But just creating a fancy structure wont be enough for you, you need to have proper design in order to withstand great pressure. The final structure will be tested with heavy weight and the one that withstands the most, WINS!

Make Best OF Waste

Make Best OF Waste! is something that we need to practise every single day. Our earth will be in great danger if we don't start acting and saving our resources today!

In this event all you need to do is create anything that is awesome right out of the waste products which will be provided to the participants. Now the stuff that you build can be literally anything and the one thing that the judges are going to consider as most useful is going to take away the prize.
